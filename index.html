Welcome

I'm an integrative counsellor/psychotherapist based in Liverpool Street and South Woodford. I aim to provide a compassionate space where individuals can begin to make sense of their inner world, helping them recognise the changes that are seeking to take place to support a more fulfilling life.

People can sometimes find themselves feeling stuck and held back by limiting patterns, moods and behaviours, which can be a source of frustration and pain. Bringing a non-judgemental awareness to feelings and thoughts can enable individuals to begin to understand old patterns more deeply, supporting them to let go of what no longer serves them. This can free up space for a new way of relating and being in the world to emerge.

Please feel free to explore this site further for more information on me and how I practise.
